Really impressed with this bundle

I've been very impressed with this bundle so far. I'm new to the PS4 and while it was a little bit tricky to navigate early on, I've gotten the hang of it and I like the interface. Downloading the games from the codes supplied was pretty easy, and while I was worried about the controller running out of charge, that hasn't been a problem at all. It's like charging a phone, and if you put the console in rest mode after you use it you should be fine to play for hours. (Though I did turn down the lights and sounds on the controller so I'm probably getting more life because of that) The console is quiet and inconspicuous and while I've only played God of War so far (which is a blast...even with some puzzles I've had to look up) the graphics are incredible. I absolutely feel like this is worth it for the money. Also, look into Playstation Now. it's amazing what titles you can download for that $9.99 monthly fee. It's basically a Netflix for games and I'm shocked by how many AAA titles they have, as well as all the classics.
